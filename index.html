Christina Liggio

Hello! My name is Christina Liggio and I am a sophomore at the University of Michigan. I am a busy college student who has a love for fitness and prioritze my health and mental well-being. I am making this page to inspire others to start putting themselves first and to excel in all aspects of life. I will highlight my academic achievements as well as my favorite workouts, recipes, tips, and inspiration.


Seven Facts About Me!

1.) I have three brothers. They have a big part in why I love to stay active. Growing up, I was always running around and playing sports outside with them. Now that we are all grown up, we still love to stay active. One of our favorite activities is tennis.

2.) I love to travel. I am blessed with parents who take the family on amazing vacations. Some of my favorites have been the lakes of Italy, an island off of Portugal called the Azores, and Saint Lucia. My dad plans our days full of activities such as kayaking and hiking. We also love to try the native foods of the places we visit!

3.) I love dogs. I have a toy poodle and a black mouth cur. These dogs also keep me active because they love to play and go on long walks.

4.) Ice cream is my go to dessert. Even on the other side of the world!

5.) My favorite forms of working out currently are Orangetheory, long walks, peloton, and yoga!

6.) My favorite activewear brand is Lululemon. I was lucky enough to work there over the holiday season!

7.) I am currently working towards my certification in personal training through the International Sports Sciences Assosiaction.
