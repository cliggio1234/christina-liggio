My Favorite Workouts and Recipes

Here are some examples of quick and effective workouts I love as well as some of my go to recipes!

Quick Full Body Workout with Dumbells


This is my favorite efficient workout for when I am low on time but want a full body burn

SUPERSET 1

* Goblet Squat x 10
* Shoulder Press x 10
* Rest
* x3

SUPERSET 2

* Weighted Glute Bridge x 15
* Chest Press while holding Glute Bridge
* Rest
* x3

SUPERSET 3

* Weighted Reverse Lunge x 16 total
* Romanian Deadlift x 10
* Rest
* x3

Dumbell Only Leg Workout


Can be done at home or in the gym!

The Circuit

* 3 X 15 reps of weighted sumo squats
* 3 x 12 (each leg) weighted single leg deadlft
* 3 x 12 weighted standng leg abduction
* 3 x 12 (each leg) weighted bulgarian split squats
* 3 x 15 weighted frog thrusts
* 3 x 12 (each leg) weighted fire hydrants

Wrap for Lunch!


Assemble the ingredients into your wrap! So quick, easy, and deliscious.

* whole wheat wrap

* 2 tablespoons hummus

* 2 ounces of turkey breast

* 1/4 cucumber thinnly sliced

* 1 roma tomato sliced thin

* 1/2 avocado sliced or mashed

* optional: lettuce

Pasta for Dinner!


Cook the pasta and veggies and combine with the lemon, olive oil, pesto, and tomatoes!

* 1 pound spaghetti

* 2 ears of corn

* 1 medium yellow squah cut into 1/2" slices

* 1 medium zucchini, cut into 1/2" slices

* 1 small bell pepper cut into sixths

* 4 green onions (trimmed)

* 2 tbsp olive oil

* 1 lemon

* 1/2 cup pesto

* 1 pint grape tomatoes halved
